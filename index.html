What is Hack Club TechBrewers?

Hack Club TechBrewers is an inclusive community which helps students from all over the world to get started with their journey in technology, irrespective of their branches, colleges, etc. We conduct sessions/ workshops on a wide variety of topics like Web Developement, AR/ VR, Personal Branding, Cyber Security and many more.
